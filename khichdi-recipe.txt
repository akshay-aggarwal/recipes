Ingredients
	1. 1 Cup of rice
	2. 1/2 Cup of moongi-chilka dal
	3. 1 Tomato
	4. 1 Onion
	5. Diced Ginger and Garlic (to taste)


Directions
	1. Slice the tomato and onion
	2. Heat the oil and cook it for 2-3 mins
	3. Add Ginger/Garlic
	4. Add rice and dal
	5. Add salt / chilli powder / turmeric powder to taste
	6. Add 3 cups of water
	7. Cook it in a cooker for 6-7 mins
